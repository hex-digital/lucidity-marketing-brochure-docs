## Why We Built Lucidity

After building numerous Sanity projects for enterprise clients, we recognized common patterns and requirements that weren't addressed by existing starters:

1. **Production-Ready from Day One**: Most starters are demos. Lucidity is built for real production use.
2. **Enterprise Requirements Built-In**: Security, governance, scalability, and team workflows are core features, not afterthoughts.
3. **Maintainable Architecture**: A well-structured monorepo with clear separation of concerns and comprehensive documentation.
4. **Best Practices Encoded**: Years of lessons learned, codified into reusable patterns and configurations.
