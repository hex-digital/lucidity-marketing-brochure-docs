Lucidity is a starter for teams building modern content-driven digital experiences with a strong foundation for growth, governance, and change.

## Why Lucidity

Lucidity helps teams move faster by starting from a well-considered architecture instead of assembling a stack from scratch.

## Who It's For

Lucidity is designed for product teams, content teams, and engineering teams that need a practical starting point without giving up long-term flexibility.

## Philosophy

The platform aims to provide sensible defaults, clear boundaries, and a path from simple implementation to enterprise-grade delivery.

## Turnkey vs Composable

Lucidity is intended to be ready-to-use on day one while still remaining open to custom workflows, integrations, and team-specific requirements over time.
