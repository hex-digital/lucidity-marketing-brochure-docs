We are [Hex Digital](https://www.hexdigital.com/?utm_source=lucidity&utm_medium=documentation-site 'Digital Agency London - Hex'),
a London-based digital agency focused on brand, website, and product delivery for organisations with
complex digital needs.

![Photo of the team at Hex Digital. There are 31 people in the photo. They look happy and smiling](/hex-team-image.webp)

With over two decades of multi-channel digital development experience, we have a deep understanding of the challenges,
risks, and pitfalls that come with building and delivering complex digital experiences.

We are one of the very first Sanity-certified partners, and we're home to one of only a few Sanity-selected MVPs. This
means we have privileged access to Sanity engineers and support engineers, and an insight into the Sanity roadmap.

**We firmly believe we are the best agency to help with Sanity enterprise projects.**

- [BirdLife International](https://datazone.birdlife.org)

- [Whirli](https://whirli.com)
- [Trustpilot](https://trustpilot.com)
- [And loads more!](https://www.hexdigital.com/our-work?utm_source=lucidity&utm_medium=documentation-site 'Our Work - Hex Digital')

These are the same classes of challenge we designed Lucidity.js to solve: multiple audiences, multiple markets, high editorial
demand, and technical quality requirements.

We offer bespoke consultancy and development services to help you build and deliver high-quality digital experiences.
This includes building and configuring custom content delivery platforms, websites, apps, products, and more. We're also
experts at migrating existing websites and rescuing projects that haven't gone to plan.
